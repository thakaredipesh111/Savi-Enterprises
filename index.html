About SAVI ENTERPRISES

SAVI ENTERPRISES is a trusted industrial fabrication and maintenance company based in Palghar, Maharashtra. Led by Mr. Vicky Thakare, we provide customized fabrication solutions for manufacturing industries, warehouses, HVAC plants, utilities and infrastructure projects. Our expertise includes fabrication, welding, piping, structural works, machine support structures, industrial trolleys, safety guarding systems, operator safety structures, ladders, platforms and industrial maintenance works.
